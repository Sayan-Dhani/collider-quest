    The real CMS detector

    The game's circular event display is a slice through this real detector, looking straight down the beam pipe. From the centre outward: silicon tracker (curved charged-particle tracks) → ECAL crystals (electrons & photons) → HCAL (jets) → the superconducting solenoid (3.8 T) → the steel return yoke threaded with muon chambers (muons). 14,000 tonnes, 15 m across — and a person for scale.
